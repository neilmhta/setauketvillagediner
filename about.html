About Us

The Setauket Village Diner is located at 238 Rt. 25A, East Setauket, NY in the Wild By Nature & Starbucks Coffee shopping center. The Setauket Village Diner has been family owned and operated since 1967. We use only the freshest ingredients and all dishes are made to order. We accomodate all and everyone's dietary needs. Our menu includes breakfast, brunch, lunch, early bird dinner, late night tapas and all-day cocktails, beer, and wines. Contact us via phone, email or visit us in person! We are open for in-person dining.
